HERE IS SOME BRIEF ON RAMEN

1. Ramen noodles in its simplest form is Chinese wheat noodles in soup broth.
2. Although introduced originally from China, Japan has created an entire cult-like obsession making ramen anything but simple.
3. In Japan, ramen shops and ramen making are considered a culinary art and Japanese people line up for hours to try a bowl made by a master ramen chef.
4. Some ramen restaurants are so popular, there are line-ups hours before the shop is even open.
5. Ramen in Japan is also quite diverse with different kinds of broths, toppings and noodle styles as well as regional variations.
6. Different areas, cities, and even shops have their own twist on ramen preparation and ingredients making each ramen experience different and unique.
